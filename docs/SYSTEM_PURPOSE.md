# System Purpose

## Overview

The General Services Office (GSO) Inventory Management System is a web-based inventory management application developed to digitize the inventory processes of the General Services Office.

The system replaces manual inventory recording and reporting by providing a centralized platform for managing government assets, generating reports, printing QR code labels, importing inventory records from Excel, and tracking inventory information through dynamic and configurable modules.

The application is designed to improve inventory accuracy, simplify asset monitoring, reduce manual work, and support the inventory reporting requirements of the General Services Office.

---

# Objectives

The system aims to:

- Digitize the inventory management process.
- Reduce manual encoding and repetitive work.
- Organize inventory records in a centralized database.
- Generate inventory reports automatically.
- Support QR Code asset identification.
- Improve asset tracking.
- Minimize inventory discrepancies.
- Simplify Excel-based data migration.
- Support configurable inventory structures through dynamic columns.
- Improve efficiency during physical inventory activities.
